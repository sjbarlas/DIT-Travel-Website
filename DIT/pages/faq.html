FAQ

1) " Where do I go if I have decided on where I want to travel?"
On this site we do not host facilities to book your trip. This site is mainly meant as a guide for those considering travel. We hope the information we provide gives you some inspiration when trying to determine the options available to you. Please check travel sites and agencies to learn more information about how and when to book a trip.

2) " What information should I check beforehand?"
Travel guides, agencies and site have excellent tips on what to avoid when going on trips. Please be sure to check beforehand information about the area and customs they may have. Be sure to heed any warnings or advice presented to you by your local travel agency or site.

3) " What rules/regulations apply when travelling?"
It is a good idea to find more information about anywhere you are considering travelling to. When travelling to certain locations you may need to apply for certain visas' or even get vaccinations. We do not provide this kind of information on our site, but you can find out by searching the internet or even visiting your local GP. Be sure to pay attention to the customs and practices of the area you are visiting, so as not to offend any locals.

4) " Where did you source this information?"
This information is meant as a travel guideline only and was correct at time of print. It was written from our own knowledge of the countries we are from as well as research on our parts.
