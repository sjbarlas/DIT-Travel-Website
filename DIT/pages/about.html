About Us

Who are we?
We are a group of five students from DIT in our second year of a Computer Science course. After many debates, we decided to create a website focusing on travel. Each one of use is from a different country, so we decided to share some of our knowledge of our own countries. We aim to provide other students an insight into the beauty and culture of our homelands. We are very proud of where we are from, and we wanted to reflect this in our work.

What is our aim?
For students of DIT there is no information about travel, so we felt the need to provide it. We feel travelling the world is very important for students of all ages and backgrounds, and we hope this is reflected in our work. We aim to provide students information about travel possibilities available to them. We provide details about travel destinations and what there is to see and do. We hope students will make use of the information we provide, and also learn about new places they may be interested in travelling to.
